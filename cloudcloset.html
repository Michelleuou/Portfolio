Cloud Closet

Description

Cloud Closet is an app for people who find it difficult to decide on what to wear that is appropriate for the weather of the day. It provides weather forecasts, gives outfit recommendations based on the weather, and allows users to mix and match their existing clothes. The app was created by two people. I collaborated with my colleague Jennifer. The apps main focus was our closet and chatbot feature Ruki.

Goal

Our target audience for our app is students and adults that need outfit suggestions based on weather. The goal of our app was to help students and adults who travel for work and school. As weather can be unpredicatble and sometimes people don't know what to wear for the day. Our app lets users choose an appropriate outfit for the weather while also letting people have their style of clothing.

Background

When we were brainstorming app ideas, we researched the tiny problems in our daily lives and heard someone said that they were having trouble deciding what to wear for the day. So we decided to create an app that helps this situation. We also considered having a weather forecast in the app to provide appropriate outfit recommendations.
